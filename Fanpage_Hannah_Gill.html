Hannah Gillians Unite!


Hannah Gill is a prolific singer focusing mainly on Jazz and alternative music. A successful musician in her own right, she is most famous for her work done with the traveling band Post-Modern Jukebox.
Her original music videos have collectively over 1 million views, and her work with PMJ has seen over 14 million views. Her powerful voice has captivated audiences from Sydney to Paris and far between.
